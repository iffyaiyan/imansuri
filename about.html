About Me

I am a Machine Learning Systems Engineer and researcher working on the design, optimization, and governance of large-scale AI systems.

My background spans NLP, Generative AI, distributed ML, and GPU-accelerated systems. I am currently pursuing research in responsible and human-centered AI with a focus on healthcare and long-term system reliability.

Philosophy

I believe modern AI progress is constrained not by models, but by systems: memory hierarchies, communication, inference orchestration, and alignment constraints.
