Research Interests

My research focuses on re-framing modern ML challenges as systems problems; where memory, communication, compute orchestration, and governance dominate performance and reliability.

  • LLM Unlearning & Model Lifecycle Control
    Studying selective forgetting mechanisms, unlearning guarantees, and system-level validation for compliance-driven AI systems.
  • ML Systems for LLM Inference
    KV-cache management, attention memory optimization, batching strategies, and inference-time scheduling.
  • Federated & Distributed Learning Systems
    Communication-efficient training, robustness under partial participation, and scaling behavior across heterogeneous devices.
  • High-Performance Computing for AI
    GPU memory hierarchy, parallel computation models, and performance bottlenecks in large-scale training and inference.
  • Responsible & Human-Centric AI Systems
    Bridging systems design with accountability, privacy, and long-term societal impact.

Open Collaboration

I am actively interested in collaborations at the intersection of ML systems research and real-world deployment; particularly where theory meets operational constraints.
